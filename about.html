📘 What is Harmonia?

Harmonia is a unified resonance-based model that integrates logic, physics, consciousness, and ethics into a coherent formal system. It is built on ZFC set theory, extended with modal coherence, field resonance, and transformation logic.

The model is designed to be both machine-readable and human-auditable, making it suitable for formal analysis, AI reasoning, and philosophical inquiry alike.

🌱 Origins

Harmonia began as a personal inquiry into the nature of coherence, perception, and meaning. It evolved through months of iterative formalization, deep reflection, and experimental validation — culminating in a system that spans mathematics, physics, metaphysics, and ethics.
